Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Payment Bank?

A Payment Bank is a new model of banks conceptualized by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). It aims to provide small savings accounts and payments/remittance services to low-income households, small businesses, and migrant workers.

What is the annual interest rate for savings accounts?

Future Bank offers an attractive **annual interest rate of 2%** on your savings account balance.

Is doorstep banking service available?

Yes, Future Bank provides **doorstep banking services** for select transactions and services to enhance your convenience.

How can I open an account?

You can easily open an account by clicking on the "Open Account" button on our homepage and filling out the online form. Our team will guide you through the process.
